12. Grow Your Connections

Now that you’ve made these targeted adjustments, it’s time to grow your connections! Get in proximity to the right people and places in tech.

Start by connecting with the Millersville University Staff & Alumni community for a vast network to draw from during your job search. Then, branch out to past co-workers, friends, family, employees of tech companies, and people met at webinars or meetups.

Pro Tip

Resist clicking “Connect” with everyone. Instead, click “Connect” then “Add a Note” for every request to reduce SPAM and increase acceptance.

Example:
“Hey John! I’m a UX/UI Design student at Millersville University and saw you speak at our event today. Thanks for sharing. I really liked your time management ideas. I’d love to connect with you here on LinkedIn!”
