6. Add Your Skills

Adding skills showcases your talents, expertise, strengths, and passions while building credibility with peers, managers, and recruiters.

Update all skills based on your resume and market analysis.

First, add any/all of these soft skills relevant to you:

  • Web Development (Soft Skills)
    • Problem Solving, Collaborative Problem Solving, Patience, Creativity, High Level of Accuracy, Communication, Empathy, Open-mindedness, Easily Adaptable, Adaptability, Critical Thinking, Accountability, Humility, Humbleness, Self-confidence, Confidence, Time Management, People Management, Project Management

Then, add these hard skills as you learn them:

  • Web Development (Hard Skills)
    • JavaScript, ES6, React, React.js, React Hooks, HTML, Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), Node.js, Express.js, AJAX, HTTPS, Mongoose, MongoDB, JSON, Git, GitHub

How To Add Skills & Endorsements

Pro Tip

Reorder your top 3 Skills to best represent you and showcase your abilities.

Bonus Tip: Get Endorsed! Endorse others’ skills, and they’ll often reciprocate, adding weight and trust to your profile.
