4. Add a Nice Profile Photo

Don’t overthink your LinkedIn profile photo. With a smartphone and a little intentionality, you can set your profile apart with the kind of profile photo that lands you interviews!

How To Add/Change Your Profile Photo

Pro Tip

Use nothing more than your smartphone to take a great photo:

  • Have good natural lighting
  • Stand about 5-6 feet away from a neutral wall
  • Hold your phone slightly higher than eye level
  • Frame in your head & shoulders
  • Look right at your camera dot and give a big, genuine smile!
  • Want to spice it up? Try pfpmaker.com

Warning

DO NOT use the #OpentoWork overlay on your profile photo. Most recruiters and hiring managers agree that it makes you look desperate and hurts your chances due to its passive nature. It also opens you to SPAM.

Instead, keep your smiling face front and center and enable the “Share with recruiters only” option to let recruiters find you appropriately.
