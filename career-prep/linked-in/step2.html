2. Create a Professional Headline

You have 6 seconds to catch a hiring manager’s attention. If they’re looking at your LinkedIn profile, you’re moments away from being put in the “must interview” pile or the “nope” pile. Confusion at this crucial moment could instantly kill your chances at the interview.

Clarity is key when writing your headline. Keep it to one line and use vertical lines to create a clean, scannable look.

Steps to Update Your Headline

  1. Update your headline
    1. Desired role or title (e.g., Software Engineer)
    2. What you will do in keywords (e.g., NodeJS, SQL, Angular, ReactJS, JavaScript, HTML/CSS)
    3. Example: Software Engineer | NodeJS | SQL | Angular | ReactJS | JavaScript | HTML/CSS
