14. Review All CTAs & Verify Contact Info

Call-to-actions (CTAs) are extremely important to make your profile easy to use. Ensure people can get a hold of you by double-checking your contact information and verifying all links.

Examples of contact information and links to check:

  • Email: Reach out directly at
  • Website: Check out what I have been working on here
  • GitHub: See my code over at my GitHub
  • Portfolio: Want to see my entire portfolio
  • Publications: As seen in this news outlet
  • Product: See my product in action here
