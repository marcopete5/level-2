8. Add Your Work Experience

Repurpose your updated and optimized resume’s content by copying and pasting it to LinkedIn. The LinkedIn search algorithm scans for key terms, just like Application Tracking Systems do for resumes.

Note: LinkedIn lacks a native bullet point system. Use symbols from this article to add bullets.
