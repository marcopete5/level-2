Introducing the Millersville University LinkedIn Guide

The fastest and easiest way to get a professional LinkedIn profile for a career in tech! This step-by-step guide will ensure you feel confident making targeted adjustments so you can put your best foot forward into your new tech career.

What You’ll Need

  • Log into LinkedIn

1. Shorten Your Custom URL

It’s important to remove as many hoops and barriers as possible for people, including hiring managers and recruiters, to find you on LinkedIn. That starts with creating a short, easy-to-remember LinkedIn URL.

Shorten your URL and you’ll have a simple, memorable link you can use on your resume, email signature, and easily share with friends and/or new networking contacts you make along the way.

How To Edit Your Custom URL

Pro Tip

Edit from: linkedin.com/in/your-name-85178123487 to: linkedin.com/in/yourname

Have a common name? Time to get creative! Try: your-name, firstmlast, theyourname, yournamethedev, yournamethedesigner, etc.
