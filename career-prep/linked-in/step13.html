13. Giving/Receiving Recommendations

A good recommendation carries significant weight. LinkedIn allows you to give and request recommendations displayed on your profile.

How To Use Recommendations Feature

Pro Tip

Think of 2–3 people you admire and “Recommend” them. Then, request recommendations from 2–3 trusted people who know your character and work ethic (peers, past managers, teachers).

Sample Recommendation Request:

Hi {name},

I hope everything’s going well at {work/location}! I’m writing to ask if you’d be willing to write a LinkedIn recommendation for me that highlights my {soft skills/qualities/keywords e.g., Team player ability}.

Ideally, if you could talk about your experience working with me through {work/experience/event} at {location if applicable}.

I’m working on {what you’re trying to accomplish e.g., New job} and a recommendation from you will significantly help my profile show a focus {what you want to be highlighted}.

I appreciate your time and I hope to hear from you soon.

Thank you,
{name}
