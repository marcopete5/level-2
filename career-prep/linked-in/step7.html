7. Update LinkedIn Settings

Quick Directions

  1. Click “Me” (top right)
  2. Click Account “Settings & Privacy”
  3. Click “Account Preferences” (top left)
  4. Scroll down (right side) and Click “People also viewed”
  5. Turn toggle to the “No” position

Check Other Settings

  1. Account preferences > Display
    • Dark mode (you’re welcome)
  2. Account preferences > People also viewed
    • Off
  3. Account preferences > Showing profile photos
    • All LinkedIn members
  4. Account preferences > Name, Location & Industry
    • Set your location & industry
  5. Visibility > Profile viewing options
    • Your name and headline
  6. Visibility > Who can see your last name
    • Select the top option (first name last name, not abbreviated)
  7. Visibility > Profile discovery using email address
    • Anyone on LinkedIn
  8. Visibility > Profile discovery using phone number
    • Everyone
  9. Data Privacy > Job seeking preferences
    • Job application settings
      • Save uploaded resumes and answers to application questions: On
      • Save self-ID information: On
  10. Data Privacy > Job seeking preferences
    • Share your profile when you click Apply for a job: On
    • Signal your interest to recruiters at companies you’ve created job alerts for: On
