1. Get Close to the Right People

To break into tech, you need to connect with people and places already doing what you aspire to do.

Start by connecting with your existing Millersville University community. You’ll be amazed at how deeply connected Millersville University is in the tech world, bringing you closer to your dream job.

Begin simply by connecting with Millersville University staff and alumni on LinkedIn and engaging in Millersville University’s Slack channels.

While building these connections, join networking groups. These groups offer a unique way to meet new people, ask behind-the-scenes questions, and hear about job openings—often before they’re posted online.

Top 3 Recommended Communities for All Students

  • FreeCodeCamp Community – Massive Online, Open-Source Platform with a Vast Community.
  • Dev.to – Blogging and Networking Platform for Developers.
  • RizeNext – A LinkedIn group for tech career growth.

Once established, join additional groups on platforms like Meetup, Slack, Facebook Groups, or LinkedIn Groups to match your goals.

Tip: Search for groups on Meetup.com using terms like “JavaScript” or “Coding,” with your zip code.

Conferences, many now online, are also great for meeting peers, growing contacts, and learning about jobs.
