Career Prep: Networking

Get in proximity to the right people and places to break into tech.

Job searching can be a stressful and daunting experience. But it doesn’t have to be that way.

Getting connected with the right people and the right places can make your job search easier and more exciting. This is where networking comes in.

Networking gives you the best chance at landing a great stepping-stone opportunity in tech. Why? Because relationships matter. At its core, networking is about building good relationships in and around your industry. Those relationships often open new doors of opportunity.

Having a strong network allows you to get closer to people at the companies you’re interested in and showcases your credibility to potential employers, making you a more trustworthy candidate.
