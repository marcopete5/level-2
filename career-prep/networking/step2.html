2. Ask Good Questions

Good networking isn’t just about growing your numbers—it’s about asking good questions.

Your network holds answers about the jobs you’re interested in, and people are often eager to share if you ask.

Questions to Ask

  • What company do you work for?
  • What do you love about your job?
  • What was your interview process like?
  • What types of people win at your company?
  • What’s the toughest question you’ve answered in an interview?
  • What’s one thing you did differently to get where you’re at?
  • What’s one thing most people don’t do that could help them get a job?
